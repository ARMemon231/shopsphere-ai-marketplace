## Delivery Issues

### Missing or Lost Packages

**If Tracking Shows Delivered But You Haven't Received:**
1. Check with neighbors and household members
2. Look in all delivery locations (porch, garage, mailroom)
3. Wait 24 hours (sometimes marked delivered early)
4. Contact us after 24 hours: support@shopsphere.com

**We Will:**
- File claim with carrier
- Issue replacement or refund within 5-7 business days
- Require police report for high-value items ($500+)

### Damaged Packages
- **Report within 48 hours** of delivery
- **Take photos** of damaged packaging and items
- **Contact us:** support@shopsphere.com
- **Keep all materials** until claim is resolved

**Resolution:**
- Free replacement shipped immediately, or
- Full refund including shipping costs

### Delayed Shipments
If your order hasn't arrived within the estimated delivery window:
1. Track your package online
2. Contact carrier directly (number in tracking email)
3. Contact us if unresolved: support@shopsphere.com

**We Will:**
- Investigate with carrier
- Provide updates every 24-48 hours
- Offer refund or replacement if not located within 7 days

## Shipping FAQs

**Q: When will my order ship?**  
A: Orders placed before 2 PM EST (Mon-Fri) ship same day. After 2 PM or on weekends ship next business day.

**Q: Can I change my shipping address after ordering?**  
A: Yes, if order hasn't shipped. Contact us immediately at support@shopsphere.com.

**Q: Do you ship to P.O. Boxes?**  
A: Standard and Expedited shipping yes. Express/Overnight shipping requires physical address.

**Q: How long does international shipping take?**  
A: Standard: 7-14 days, Express: 3-5 days. Times may vary by customs.

**Q: Who pays customs duties for international orders?**  
A: Customer is responsible for all customs duties, taxes, and fees.

**Q: Can I track my international order?**  
A: Yes, all international orders include tracking.

**Q: What if my package is lost?**  
A: Contact us immediately. We'll file a claim and arrange replacement or refund.

**Q: Do you offer Saturday delivery?**  
A: Yes, available with Express shipping for additional $15 fee.

**Q: Can I pick up my order?**  
A: We don't have retail locations, but you can choose "Hold at Location" for carrier pickup.
