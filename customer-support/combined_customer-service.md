## Product Care & Maintenance

### Watch Care

**Daily Care:**
- Wipe with soft cloth after wearing
- Avoid extreme temperatures
- Remove before showering (unless 50M+ rated)
- Store in watch box when not in use

**Maintenance:**
- Battery replacement: Every 2-3 years
- Leather strap care: Avoid water, condition monthly
- Water resistance check: Annually
- Professional service: Every 3-5 years

### Earbuds Care

**Cleaning:**
- Clean ear tips weekly with mild soap
- Wipe earbuds with dry cloth
- Clean charging contacts monthly
- Use soft brush for speaker mesh

**Storage:**
- Always store in charging case
- Avoid extreme temperatures
- Keep dry
- Don't expose to direct sunlight

**Battery Care:**
- Charge regularly
- Don't let battery fully drain often
- Avoid overcharging (though cases prevent this)
- Expected lifespan: 2-3 years

### Phone Case Care

**Cleaning:**
- Remove case weekly for cleaning
- Wash with mild soap and water
- Dry completely before reinstalling
- Avoid harsh chemicals

**Maintenance:**
- Check for wear regularly
- Replace if cracked or damaged
- Clean port covers on armor cases
- Condition leather cases monthly

# Example with LangChain
from langchain.document_loaders import DirectoryLoader
from langchain.text_splitter import MarkdownHeaderTextSplitter
from langchain.embeddings import OpenAIEmbeddings

headers_to_split_on = [
