## Order Management

### Placing an Order

**Step-by-Step:**
1. Browse products and add to cart
2. Review cart and proceed to checkout
3. Enter shipping information
4. Choose shipping method
5. Enter payment information
6. Review order summary
7. Place order

**Payment Methods Accepted:**
- Credit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex, Discover)
- Debit Cards
- PayPal (coming soon)
- Apple Pay (coming soon)
- Cash on Delivery (select areas)

### Order Confirmation

**What to Expect:**
- Immediate order confirmation email
- Order number for tracking
- Estimated delivery date
- Order summary with items

**Didn't Receive Confirmation?**
- Check spam/junk folder
- Allow 15 minutes for email delivery
- Contact support if still not received

### Tracking Your Order

**How to Track:**
1. Check shipping confirmation email
2. Click tracking link, or
3. Visit www.shopsphere.com/track
4. Enter order number

**Tracking Updates:**
- Order Received
- Processing
- Shipped (with tracking number)
- In Transit
- Out for Delivery
- Delivered

### Order Modifications

**Before Shipment:**
Can modify:
- Shipping address
- Shipping method (may incur fees)
- Add items (new order required)

Cannot modify:
- Remove items (must cancel order)
- Change product details

**Contact:** support@shopsphere.com immediately

**After Shipment:**
Cannot modify. Contact carrier for delivery intercept (fees apply)

### Order Cancellation

**Before Shipment:**
- Email orders@shopsphere.com with order number
- Call 1-800-ShopSphere-SHOP
- Response within 2 hours
- Full refund if cancelled before shipping

**After Shipment:**
- Cannot cancel
- Must follow return process
- Refuse delivery for faster refund

## Payment & Billing

### Payment Security

**How We Protect You:**
- 256-bit SSL encryption
- PCI DSS compliant
- Tokenized payment storage
- Fraud detection systems
- Secure checkout process

**We Never:**
- Store complete credit card numbers
- Share payment information
- Charge without authorization

### Payment Authorization

**When Charged:**
- Authorization hold at order placement
- Actual charge when order ships
- Pre-authorization may appear as pending

**Authorization Failures:**
- Insufficient funds
- Incorrect card information
- Expired card
- Bank decline

**Resolution:**
- Update payment method
- Contact your bank
- Try alternative payment method

### Promotional Codes

**How to Use:**
1. Add items to cart
2. Proceed to checkout
3. Enter code in "Promo Code" field
4. Click "Apply"
5. Discount reflected in total

**Common Issues:**
- Code expired
- Already used
- Doesn't apply to sale items
- Minimum purchase not met
- One code per order

**Can't Apply Code?**
Contact support@shopsphere.com with code and order details

### Billing Questions

**Invoice Requests:**
Email: billing@shopsphere.com
Include: Order number and email address

**Billing Address:**
Must match credit card billing address for security

**International Billing:**
Charged in USD. Bank may convert to local currency.

## Returns & Exchanges

### Return Policy Summary

**Time Frame:** 30 days from delivery
**Condition:** Unused, original packaging
**Refund Method:** Original payment method
**Processing Time:** 5-7 business days

Full details: See Return Policy document

### How to Return

1. Login to your account
2. Go to "My Orders"
3. Select order and click "Return Item"
4. Choose reason for return
5. Print return label (emailed)
6. Ship item back

**Without Account:**
Email returns@shopsphere.com with order number

### Exchange Process

We don't offer direct exchanges. Process:
1. Return original item
2. Receive refund
3. Place new order

**Benefits:**
- 10% discount code for replacement
- Priority return processing
- Free expedited shipping on replacement

### Refund Status

**Timeline:**
- Return received: 1-2 days (email sent)
- Inspection: 2-3 business days
- Refund approved: Immediate notification
- Refund processed: 5-7 business days
- Bank posting: 3-5 business days additional

**Check Status:**
- Email returns@shopsphere.com
- Call 1-800-ShopSphere-SHOP
- Include order number and return tracking

## Warranty Service

### Filing a Warranty Claim

**Requirements:**
1. Within warranty period
2. Proof of purchase (order number)
3. Description of issue
4. Photos/videos of problem

**Process:**
1. Email warranty@shopsphere.com
2. Include above information
3. Receive claim number within 24 hours
4. Ship item (free label provided)
5. Receive replacement or repair

**Turnaround Time:**
- Inspection: 2-3 business days
- Repair: 5-10 business days
- Replacement: 3-5 business days

### What's Covered

**Watches:**
- Movement defects
- Case/bracelet defects
- Crystal defects
- Manufacturer defects

**Earbuds:**
- Battery failure
- Charging issues
- Connection problems
- Audio defects

**Phone Cases:**
- Material defects
- Fit issues
- Manufacturing flaws

### What's Not Covered

**All Products:**
- Physical damage
- Water damage (non-rated items)
- Normal wear and tear
- Modifications
- Misuse
- Lost or stolen items

## Common Issues & Solutions

### Issue: Order Not Received

**Steps:**
1. Check tracking status
2. Verify delivery address
3. Check with neighbors/family
4. Wait 24 hours (sometimes marked early)
5. Contact support if not found

**We Will:**
- File carrier claim
- Issue replacement or refund
- Resolve within 5-7 business days

### Issue: Wrong Item Received

**Steps:**
1. Take photos of received item
2. Don't discard packaging
3. Contact support immediately
4. Email support@shopsphere.com

**We Will:**
- Send correct item (free expedited)
- Provide prepaid return label
- No restocking fees

### Issue: Defective Product

**Steps:**
1. Document defect with photos/video
2. Contact support within 48 hours
3. Email warranty@shopsphere.com

**We Will:**
- Offer replacement or refund
- Provide prepaid return label
- Process in 3-5 business days

### Issue: Payment Problem

**Steps:**
1. Check card information
2. Verify billing address
3. Contact your bank
4. Try alternative payment

**We Can Help:**
- Retry payment manually
- Offer alternative payment options
- Hold order while resolving

### Issue: Can't Track Order

**Steps:**
1. Check spam folder for shipping email
2. Wait 24-48 hours after order
3. Visit www.shopsphere.com/track
4. Contact support with order number

**We Will:**
- Resend tracking information
- Provide shipping status
- Investigate with carrier
