### Watch Features Explained

**Water Resistance:**
- 30M (3ATM): Splash resistant, hand washing
- 50M (5ATM): Swimming, showering
- 100M (10ATM): Swimming, snorkeling
- 200M+ (20ATM): Diving

**Movement Types:**
- **Quartz:** Battery-powered, accurate, low maintenance
- **Automatic:** Self-winding, no battery, watch enthusiast favorite
- **Mechanical:** Manual winding, traditional craftsmanship

**Crystal Types:**
- **Mineral:** Standard, scratch-resistant
- **Sapphire:** Premium, highly scratch-resistant
- **Acrylic:** Budget-friendly, easy to polish

## Product Care & Maintenance

### Watch Care

**Daily Care:**
- Wipe with soft cloth after wearing
- Avoid extreme temperatures
- Remove before showering (unless 50M+ rated)
- Store in watch box when not in use

**Maintenance:**
- Battery replacement: Every 2-3 years
- Leather strap care: Avoid water, condition monthly
- Water resistance check: Annually
- Professional service: Every 3-5 years

### Earbuds Care

**Cleaning:**
- Clean ear tips weekly with mild soap
- Wipe earbuds with dry cloth
- Clean charging contacts monthly
- Use soft brush for speaker mesh

**Storage:**
- Always store in charging case
- Avoid extreme temperatures
- Keep dry
- Don't expose to direct sunlight

**Battery Care:**
- Charge regularly
- Don't let battery fully drain often
- Avoid overcharging (though cases prevent this)
- Expected lifespan: 2-3 years

### Phone Case Care

**Cleaning:**
- Remove case weekly for cleaning
- Wash with mild soap and water
- Dry completely before reinstalling
- Avoid harsh chemicals

**Maintenance:**
- Check for wear regularly
- Replace if cracked or damaged
- Clean port covers on armor cases
- Condition leather cases monthly

## Size & Compatibility Guide

### Watch Sizing

**Wrist Size Guide:**
- **Small:** 5.5" - 6.5" (140mm - 165mm)
- **Medium:** 6.5" - 7.5" (165mm - 190mm)
- **Large:** 7.5" - 8.5" (190mm - 215mm)

**How to Measure:**
1. Use flexible measuring tape
2. Wrap around wrist bone
3. Add 1/2 inch for comfort

**Adjustments:**
- Leather straps: Free sizing with buckle
- Metal bracelets: Free link removal/addition
- Mail-in service available

### Earbuds Fit

**Ear Tip Sizes:**
- **Small:** Best for smaller ear canals
- **Medium:** Most common, default size
- **Large:** For larger ear canals or extra secure fit

**Fit Test:**
- Should feel secure but comfortable
- No pressure or pain
- Good noise isolation
- Doesn't fall out with head movement

### Phone Case Compatibility

**Important:**
- Cases are model-specific
- Not interchangeable between models
- Check your exact model before ordering
- Camera cutout must match your phone

**How to Find Your Model:**
- **iPhone:** Settings > General > About
- **Samsung:** Settings > About Phone > Model Number
