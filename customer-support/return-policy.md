## Return Policy Overview

At ShopSphere, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we offer a hassle-free return policy to ensure your shopping experience is exceptional.

### Our Promise
- **30-Day Return Window** for most items
- **Free Return Shipping** on defective or damaged items
- **Full Refund** processed within 5-7 business days
- **No Questions Asked** return policy on eligible items

---

## Eligibility for Returns

To be eligible for a return, your item must meet the following conditions:

### General Requirements
1. **Time Frame**: Items must be returned within 30 days of delivery
2. **Condition**: Items must be unused, unworn, and in original condition
3. **Packaging**: Original packaging, tags, and accessories must be included
4. **Receipt**: Proof of purchase (order number or receipt) is required

### Specific Product Requirements

#### Watches
- Must be unworn with all protective stickers intact
- Original box, warranty card, and documentation included
- Links not removed or adjusted
- No scratches or signs of wear

#### Wireless Earbuds (Airbuds)
- Original sealed packaging unopened, OR
- If opened, must show no signs of use
- All accessories (charging case, tips, cables) included
- No software pairing or device connection history

#### Phone Cases
- Original packaging intact
- No signs of installation or use
- All protective films in place
- No scratches, dents, or modifications

## Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned for hygiene, safety, or policy reasons:

### Cannot Be Returned
1. **Personalized or Customized Items**
   - Engraved watches
   - Custom phone cases with personal designs
   - Special order items

2. **Items Purchased on Final Sale**
   - Clearance items marked "Final Sale"
   - Items purchased with discount codes over 50% off

3. **Used or Damaged Items**
   - Items showing signs of wear or use
   - Items with missing parts or accessories
   - Items damaged by customer misuse

4. **Software or Digital Products**
   - Downloadable content
   - Activated warranty cards

5. **Gift Cards**
   - Cannot be returned or refunded

## Return Process

Follow these simple steps to return your item:

### Step 1: Initiate Return Request
1. Log into your ShopSphere account at www.shopsphere.com
2. Go to "My Orders" section
3. Select the order containing the item you wish to return
4. Click "Request Return" and select the reason
5. You'll receive a return confirmation email within 24 hours

**Without an Account:**
- Email: returns@shopsphere.com
- Include: Order number, item name, and reason for return
- Response within 24-48 hours

### Step 2: Prepare Your Package
1. Place the item in its original packaging
2. Include all accessories, manuals, and documentation
3. Print and include the return label (emailed to you)
4. Seal the package securely

**Packing Tips:**
- Use original box when possible
- Add extra padding for fragile items (watches)
- Do not write or place stickers on original product packaging
- Keep a photo of the packed item for your records

### Step 3: Ship Your Return
**Free Return Shipping (Defective/Damaged Items):**
- Use the prepaid return label provided
- Drop off at any authorized shipping location
- Track your return using the tracking number provided

**Standard Return Shipping:**
- Return shipping costs: $9.99 (deducted from refund)
- Use our prepaid label or your preferred carrier
- We recommend insured shipping for high-value items
- Keep tracking number until refund is processed

### Step 4: Return Processing
1. **Package Received**: We'll email you when we receive your return
2. **Inspection**: Items inspected within 2-3 business days
3. **Approval**: Return approved or denied via email
4. **Refund**: Processed within 5-7 business days of approval

## Refund Policy

### Refund Method
Refunds are issued to the original payment method used for purchase:

**Credit/Debit Card:**
- Refund processed in 5-7 business days
- May take additional 3-5 days to appear on statement
- Original shipping charges are non-refundable (unless item is defective)

**Cash on Delivery (COD):**
- Refund via bank transfer or store credit
- Bank transfer: 7-10 business days
- Store credit: Issued immediately upon approval

### Refund Calculation

**Example 1 - Standard Return:**
```
Original Order Total:        $329.99
Original Shipping:            $9.99
----------------------------------------
Subtotal:                   $339.98

Return Item Cost:          -$329.99
Return Shipping Fee:         -$9.99
----------------------------------------
Refund Amount:               $0.00
(Or $320.00 with free return shipping)
```

**Example 2 - Defective Item:**
```
Original Order Total:        $329.99
Original Shipping:            $9.99
----------------------------------------
Subtotal:                   $339.98

Return Item Cost:          -$329.99
Return Shipping Fee:          $0.00 (FREE)
Original Shipping Refund:    -$9.99
----------------------------------------
Refund Amount:              $339.98 (FULL)
```

### Partial Refunds
Partial refunds may be issued if:
- Item shows signs of use or wear
- Original packaging is damaged or missing
- Accessories or documentation not included
- Return initiated after 30-day window (discretionary)

## Damaged or Defective Items

We inspect all items before shipping, but sometimes issues occur during transit.

### What Qualifies as Damaged/Defective?

**Damaged Items:**
- Broken or cracked screens/cases
- Dented or scratched watches
- Crushed packaging with product damage
- Items damaged during shipping

**Defective Items:**
- Watch not functioning properly
- Earbuds won't charge or pair
- Phone case doesn't fit specified model
- Manufacturing defects

### Damaged/Defective Item Process

**Immediate Action Required:**
1. **Document**: Take photos/videos of damage immediately
2. **Report**: Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
3. **Don't Discard**: Keep all packaging and materials

**Contact Methods:**
- Email: support@shopsphere.com (include photos)
- Phone: 1-800-ShopSphere-SHOP (Mon-Fri, 9AM-6PM EST)
- Live Chat: Available on our website

**Resolution Options:**
1. **Full Refund** - Including original shipping
2. **Free Replacement** - Expedited shipping at no cost
3. **Store Credit** - 110% of original purchase price

**Processing Time:**
- Report reviewed within 24 hours
- Resolution offered within 48 hours
- Refund/replacement processed within 3-5 business days

## International Returns

We accept returns from international customers with some modifications:

### International Return Policy

**Return Window:** 45 days (extended from 30 days)

**Return Shipping:**
- Customer responsible for return shipping costs
- We recommend tracked and insured shipping
- Customs duties and taxes are non-refundable

**Refund Processing:**
- Same process as domestic returns
- May take 10-14 business days due to international processing
- Currency exchange rates applied at time of original purchase

**Customs Information:**
- Mark package as "RETURNED MERCHANDISE"
- Include original order number on customs form
- Value should be listed as "RETURN - NO COMMERCIAL VALUE"

## Return FAQs

### Q: Can I return an item without the original packaging?
**A:** While we prefer items in original packaging, we may accept returns without it. However, a partial refund (10-20% restocking fee) may apply.

### Q: How long does it take to receive my refund?
**A:** Once we receive and approve your return, refunds are processed in 5-7 business days. It may take an additional 3-5 days to appear on your statement.

### Q: Can I return a gift?
**A:** Yes! Gift recipients can return items for store credit. Original purchaser can return for refund to original payment method.

### Q: What if I lost my receipt/order number?
**A:** No problem! Email support@shopsphere.com with your name and email used for purchase. We'll locate your order.

### Q: Can I return a sale or clearance item?
**A:** Sale items can be returned unless marked "Final Sale." Clearance items marked "Final Sale" cannot be returned.

### Q: What if my return is denied?
**A:** We'll email you with the reason. You can appeal by contacting support@shopsphere.com with additional information.

### Q: Do you offer free return shipping?
**A:** Free return shipping is provided for defective or damaged items. Standard returns have a $9.99 return shipping fee.

### Q: Can I return an item I bought with a discount code?
**A:** Yes, but the refund will be based on the discounted price you paid.
